About the project

For the 245th season of the Bolshoi Theater, Van Cleef & Arpels presented the premiere of the ballet-opera Zephyr and Flora, or Metamorphoses.

In 1817, the ballet Zephyr and Flora was staged at the Pashkov House, and 204 years later it was rethought and performed on the stage of the Pashkov House by the famous soloists of the Bolshoi Theater.

Our Event Bureau team ensured the full-scale organization and seamless execution of the evening: from staging original ballet and opera performances to managing the 360° gala dinner production.

For the 245th season of the Bolshoi Theatre, Van Cleef & Arpels premiered the ballet-opera Zephyr and Flora, or Metamorphoses. We staged the evening in the round — a central double-sided stage divided the hall into two performance realms: ballet on one side, high jewellery defile on the other.
