About the project

For the 245th season of the Bolshoi Theater, Van Cleef & Arpels presented the premiere of the ballet-opera Zephyr and Flora, or Metamorphoses.

In 1817, the ballet Zephyr and Flora was staged at the Pashkov House, and 204 years later it was rethought and performed on the stage of the Pashkov House by the famous soloists of the Bolshoi Theater.

Our Event Bureau team ensured the full-scale organization and seamless execution of the evening: from staging original ballet and opera performances to managing the 360° gala dinner production.

For the 245th season of the Bolshoi Theatre, Van Cleef & Arpels premiered the ballet-opera Zephyr and Flora, or Metamorphoses. We staged the evening in the round — a central double-sided stage divided the hall into two performance realms: ballet on one side, high jewellery defile on the other.

Our method
1

Concept — Defining the Framework

We developed the creative concept from the ground up, establishing the intellectual and aesthetic foundation of the project.

2

Reading the Space — Constraint as a Driver

The venue became both a starting point and a challenge: a space with strict rules and limitations, yet filled with inspiration and grandeur. This required a precise and respectful approach, where constraints shaped the creative direction.

3

Scenography — Architecture as a Guide

The purity of colour, the natural light of the atrium, and the clarity of architectural lines led to an intentionally ascetic scenography — a white canvas designed for speakers and broadcast.

4

System Thinking — Designing for Dual Reality

We created a full scenographic system for both live and online formats, anticipating how the space would function simultaneously on camera and in physical presence.

5

Dramaturgy — Structuring Time and Attention

We built the dialogue as a precise narrative arc, sustaining audience engagement throughout a five-hour broadcast.

6

Visual Unity — One Language Across All Media

All digital content — from openers to speaker streams — was conceived as a single aesthetic manifesto, ensuring a seamless dialogue between online and offline participants.

7

Experience Design — The Audience Journey

The entire audience journey was constructed through our proprietary Method, where each moment is intentionally designed and conceptually grounded.
